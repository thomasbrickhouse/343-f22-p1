WHO WE ARE

We are a professional soccer club based in Washington, D.C. competing in the Eastern Conference of Major League Soccer (MLS), the top tier of American soccer. Over our 28-year history, we have won four MLS Cups, four Supporters Shields, three U.S. Open Cups, and six Eastern Conference championships. In addition, internationally, we have won one CONCACAF Champions League title and one Copa Interamericana, the only American team to win the latter.
